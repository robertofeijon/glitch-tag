// your journey

High school → dream job

Here's what the path actually looks like.

📚
Right now · High School
Start learning to code
Python on freeCodeCamp, Khan Academy, or Scratch. Build something small. Anything. A calculator, a quiz game, a website for your favourite band.
🎓
Year 1–4 · University or Bootcamp
Get your foundation
CS degree, a bootcamp, or self-teaching online — all three work. The key is building real projects and a portfolio of work you can show people.
🌱
Age 20–22 · First Job
Junior Developer
Your first real role. $60–95k. You'll feel out of your depth — that's normal. You learn faster on the job than anywhere else.
⚡
Age 25–28 · Levelling Up
Mid-level / Senior Engineer
Lead features, mentor others, make architectural calls. $120–180k. You can now pick the company, the stack, even the country you work in.
🚀
Age 30+ · The Horizon
Principal Engineer · Manager · Founder
Lead entire engineering orgs, build your own startup, or become a specialist worth $300k+. The ceiling is genuinely high in this field.
